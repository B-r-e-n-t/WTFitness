Your Health is Your Wealth

Personal Training that comes to you

James Corbett

Personal Training in Austin, TX

Welcome! I am a passionate fitness enthusiast and a certified personal trainer from the prestigious Cooper's Institute, with over 12 years of dedicated experience in guiding individuals towards their health and fitness goals. My journey in the fitness industry has been driven by a single powerful belief: your health is your wealth.
As an ACE Certified Group X Instructor, I thrive in creating dynamic and engaging group fitness experiences, ensuring that every session is not just a workout, but a step towards a healthier, more vibrant lifestyle. My expertise is further enhanced by specialized certifications in Les Mills CXWORX and Les Mills Body Pump, allowing me to offer a diverse range of fitness programs tailored to meet individual needs and preferences.
With a CF-L1 certification, I bring the intensity and community spirit of functional fitness to my clients, ensuring that each training session is as rewarding as it is challenging. My approach is holistic and personalized, focusing on building strength, endurance, and mobility that translates beyond the gym and into everyday life.
Being in the fitness industry as both a trainer and group exercise instructor for over a decade isn't just a career; it's a testament to my dedication to this lifestyle. I love what I do, and nothing brings me more joy than seeing the transformative impact of fitness on my clients' lives. Every day, I am privileged to help someone reach their goals, witnessing firsthand how investing in health can enrich all aspects of life.
Join me on this journey to discover the wealth in your health, one empowering workout at a time.
